Our Roof Boxes

The Thule 200 Roof Box is a versatile and reliable cargo solution designed to expand your vehicle’s storage capacity with ease. Engineered for everyday use and weekend adventures alike, this sleek and aerodynamic roof box offers ample space for luggage, camping gear, sports equipment, and more, without compromising your car’s performance or fuel efficiency.

With a 200-liter storage volume, the Thule 200 is ideal for all vehicle types, providing plenty of extra room. Its thoughtfully designed low profile reduces drag and wind noise, while the weather-resistant construction ensures your belongings stay dry and protected in all conditions.

Installation is simple and secure thanks to Thule’s intuitive mounting system, which fits most standard roof bars. The dual-side opening makes loading and unloading effortless. Built with durable materials and trusted engineering, the Thule 200 roof box combines practicality, style, and durability — perfect for camping trips, family holidays, ski runs, and everything in between.
